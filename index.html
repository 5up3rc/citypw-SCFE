SCFE is an Open Educational Project.

** Who will be interested in SCFE? Programming beginner, Compute Science/Engineer student or anyone who want to learn computer programming.

** What's in SCFE? Any "hello world" code/docs in all programming area. You may learn the sample code for any language you're instered in. You may find the simplest operating system or web server or compiler... You'll get what you want in your first time study it.

** So, tell me what is SCFE? Well, maybe you want to know how to write a Game, and you have no idea, no books, no sample code. You don't know anything about what you're going to learn. And SCFE will give you ABC about your target. Nevertheless, it's not only Game. Anything computer programming involved.

** I know something, can I be a contributor? Yes! Of course! Please make sure that your code/docs is easy to learn. The simplest is the best. But it doesn't mean everything keep in print "hello world" level. "hello world" is a metaphor, which means "once you see it, you understand it".
